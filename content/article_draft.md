### The "Chat Trap"
Most people treat AI like a consultant. You go to a website (ChatGPT, Claude), you type a question, and it gives you a brilliant answer.
Then you copy the code. You switch windows. You paste it. You run it. It errors. You copy the error. You paste it back...

This is the **Chat Loop**. It feels productive, but you are still the bottleneck. You are the manual data entry clerk for your own AI.
